ABOUT US

Dobrobat focuses on the initial reconstruction of damaged buildings. The project's priority is "fast recovery," or rapid restoration: re-roofing damaged roofs, repairing broken windows, and rebuilding damaged walls.

Volunteer centres are located in Kyiv, Chernihiv, Sumy, Kharkiv, Mykolaiv, Zaporizhzhia, Odesa and Kherson Oblasts

OUR MISSSION & GOALS

  • 01
    We want to help internally displaced people to return home and avoid becoming refugees
  • 02
    We want Connect global construction industry and global Ukraine supporters to the volunteers on the ground in Ukraine who restore damaged buildings and infrastructure
  • 03
    Restore 50,000 homes and 500 social infrastructure buildings (schools, kindergartens, urgent care facilities). We are >20% there now.
  • 04
    To inspire hope and resilience by bringing activities together to rebuild, recover and help as many ukrainians affected by the war stay home with the help of dedicated volunteers and partners
